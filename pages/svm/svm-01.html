SVM 01 — What is Space Vector Modulation?

Goal: synthesize a desired voltage vector using inverter switching states efficiently.

On this page 1) The idea 2) Hexagon preview Next

The idea

A 3-phase inverter has discrete switching states. Each state corresponds to a voltage vector in the αβ plane. SVM chooses a sequence of states (and durations) so that the average voltage equals your desired vector.
