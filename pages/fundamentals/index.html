Fundamentals — The story before SVM and FOC

Many tutorials jump straight into SVM or FOC and the beginner gets lost. Here we build the chain step by step: what we want (speed/torque) → how motors create it (current) → how we influence current (voltage) → what hardware can do (inverter ON/OFF) → how ON/OFF becomes “analog” (PWM) → how we visualize it (αβ).

Where this leads

After these fundamentals:

  • SVM becomes “obvious”: it is just a smart way to do PWM in a 3-phase inverter.
  • FOC becomes “clear”: it is a control strategy that chooses the right rotating vector to get the torque you want.
